Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to gain an online precense for this business that I'm kickstarting, I'll continue to not oly grow the business, but also improve any bugs on the site. So if you experience any, feel free to contact us.

Audience

The audience being referenced is of course those interested in actually white water rafting and for those who are open to doing business as well. Whether its a business like our that is just starting, or an older one, we are all ears! On our side, we look to provide the gear required to raft, including vests, paddles, the raft itself, and even swimsuits too. We also provide lockerrooms for customers to store their belongings, we will have employees on standby along the river along with some at our home base to provide the customers a safe enviroment and experience. We are looking forward to meeting you all!
